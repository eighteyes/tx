When creating tailored materials:

1. **Mine this document** for specific stories and examples that match job requirements
2. **Use actual metrics** and outcomes from the detailed project descriptions
3. **Adapt stories** to highlight skills mentioned in job posting
4. **Reference specific projects** that align with company's tech stack
5. **Pull keywords** from the skills bank that match the JD
6. **Use context** to understand progression and growth over time
7. **Leverage interview stories** to strengthen cover letter narratives
8. **Match tone** to company culture (startup vs. enterprise, formal vs. casual)
